Why Portugal

Remote worker friendly & affordable

Porto is one of the most popular cities for remote workers and Portugal is very affordable to live with median rents at $700/mo which is 63% lower than the median rent in Europe. Food and other expenses are also much cheaper. The weather is mild to warm all year round. There's a huge social scene of int'l people. And it's geographically central: only an 11-hour flight to Bangkok and a 6-hour flight to New York. This makes it a great place for remote workers and nomads to set up their base from.

Highly walkable for a healthy life

With its mild year-round climate and clean air, great trails, pathways, Portugal is ideal for active people looking to walk, go for hikes or running.

Friendliest people in the world

Expats rate Portuguese as the friendliest people in the world.

Fluent English speakers

Portuguese are ranked #7 in English profiency putting them in the top of non-native English speakers worldwide.
